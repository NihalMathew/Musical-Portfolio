BIO

Nihal Mathew (b. 1987) is a composer living in Chennai, Tamilnadu. .

He started his journey in music at an early age of 12. He is passionate about composing music. He released his first composition at an early age of 17 years, which became a huge success in college. The college called it "a mighty ear-stunner ... exhilarating to experience."

Nihal has composed music for several short films that became viral in youtube. Some of his works include "Poi kaal kuthirai", "Unai Naan", "kaalajaalam",etc. He has worked with noticeable directors from the industry including Maria Elenchezian who refers to him as a "5 minute wonder man"

He recieved an appreciation award for his recent work in "Poi kaal kuthirai" from the college Dean.
